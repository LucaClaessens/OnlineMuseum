Mission Statement

The ______ is an online platform which functions as both a museum and as an on-going research project into ways that online museums can be congured. The platform will present dierent ways that content (collections- objects, artworks etc) can be displayed online. It is an on-going exploration into ways a ‘museum’ can function when it exists within the digital realm and is located online. The _____ is a framework, which expands with every new contribution for how the online museum could be.

The content for the ____ is ‘borrowed’ from other museums, who have uploaded images of their collections to the internet, with creative commons licencing. Collections and artworks can merge, away from institutional practices.
